Welcome to Rajasthan - The Land of Kings

Rajasthan, the largest state of India, is renowned for its vibrant culture, rich heritage, and majestic forts and palaces. Situated in the northwestern part of the country, Rajasthan is a land of contrasts, where the golden sands of the Thar Desert meet the lush greenery of the Aravalli Range.

History of Rajasthan

The history of Rajasthan dates back to ancient times, with its roots deeply intertwined with the rise and fall of various dynasties and kingdoms. From the mighty Rajputs to the Mughals and the Marathas, Rajasthan has been ruled by several powerful rulers who have left behind a legacy of grandeur and opulence.

Explore Rajasthan

Today, Rajasthan is a treasure trove of architectural wonders, with magnificent palaces, majestic forts, and intricately carved temples that bear testimony to its glorious past. Each city in Rajasthan has its own unique charm, from the vibrant streets of Jaipur, the pink city, to the serene lakes of Udaipur, the city of lakes.
